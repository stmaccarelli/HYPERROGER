ROGER WATER is a web-based interactive and audioreactive 360 + VR experience that goes far beyond the concept of a VR or interactive music video. It's a kind of explorative videogame.

It's based on HYPERLAND, the VR AV show of NIAGARA. Point here for more informations and booking.

The landscapes are generated, animated and populated in real time, thanks to sound analysis, as you explore them. You can travel that world with a computer or a smartphone, in 360 or VR (Cardboard) mode.

You can fly freely in this infinite and everchanging generative open world, accelerate, fly up or down, follow creatures, or just stay and contemplate the environment.
You can also manually populate the world by launching objects and creatures, and Cardboard mode is compatible with bluetooth VR remote controllers.

Experiencing ROGER WATER world from a computer or Android device gives you the chance to activate your device's microphone and let the world react to your voice, music, ambient sounds, etc. You just need to wait for ROGER WATER music to end.

The generative world may also react with your time and location, and features some secret things that players can discover by finding "special areas" or by visiting ROGER WATER world at specific hours.
New features will be launched and announced on Niagara and Stefano Maccarelli social network profiles.
