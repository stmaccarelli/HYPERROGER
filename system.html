INIT DIV

loading progress then enable "START BUTTON", that hides screen and plays audio. 

	COMMANDS:
		H, Y, P, E, R = launch objects
		mouse = move
		mouse button click / hold = accelerate
		spacebar = pause

PAUSED DIV

resume, back to home 

		COMMANDS:
			H, Y, P, E, R = launch objects
			mouse = move
			mouse button click / hold = accelerate
			spacebar = pause

ENDING DIV

choose play again or mic PLAY AGAIN
